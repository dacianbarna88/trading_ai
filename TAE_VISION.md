# TAE Vision

**Trading AI Ecosystem (TAE)**

---

## What TAE Is

TAE is a **living research ecosystem** — not a trading bot, not an execution engine, and not a dashboard glued to a broker.

TAE is the scientific and organizational foundation for how Trading AI discovers, validates, stores, debates, and evolves knowledge about markets before any capital is risked. It treats research as a continuous organism: observing, questioning, learning, teaching, and adapting.

Execution is a late-stage consumer of TAE output — never the starting point.

---

## What TAE Is Not

| TAE is not… | Because… |
|-------------|------------|
| A live trading bot | Bots execute; TAE researches |
| A signal spammer | TAE requires evidence and validation |
| A black box | Every score must be explainable |
| A broker integration | Broker is last, not first |
| A single script | TAE is modular, layered, and evolutionary |

---

## Core Philosophy

TAE orders priorities explicitly:

1. **Research before execution** — No order flow without a research trail.
2. **Evidence before opinion** — Hypotheses must produce dossiers, not vibes.
3. **Validation before trust** — Walk-forward, robustness, and ensemble checks precede promotion.
4. **Knowledge before profit** — Profit is a lagging indicator of good knowledge.
5. **Collective intelligence before individual decisions** — Organisms debate; no single module rules alone.
6. **Broker is last, not first** — Connectivity follows validated paper decision systems.

These principles are non-negotiable for every sprint, organism, and roadmap phase.

## The TAE Motto

> **We never stop learning.**  
> **We never stop moving.**  
> **We never stop questioning.**  
> **We never stop teaching.**  
> **We never stop evolving.**

Learning is continuous — not a one-time backtest. Movement is intellectual and architectural — research pipelines advance even when markets are closed. Questioning is structural — every edge has a failure mode. Teaching is explicit — explanations are outputs, not afterthoughts. Evolution is governed — weak ideas archive; robust ideas earn trust.

---

## The Problem TAE Solves

Traditional trading stacks often:

- Jump from indicator → trade without validation layers
- Store wins but not **why** they worked
- Promote strategies on in-sample hype
- Silo context, momentum, risk, and evidence in unrelated scripts
- Treat the broker as the center of gravity

TAE inverts this. The center of gravity is the **Knowledge Core** — a durable, auditable memory of what the ecosystem has observed, validated, doubted, and learned.

---

## Vision Statement

**Build a self-improving research civilization inside Trading AI** where specialized organisms observe different facets of the market, produce evidence, challenge one another, and deposit only validated knowledge into a shared core — so that when paper or live systems eventually act, they act on collective intelligence, not isolated code.

---

## Success Looks Like

- Every historical signal has a human-readable evidence dossier.
- Every promoted edge has validation history, trust score, and failure conditions.
- Researchers (human or automated) can trace **why** a decision label was assigned.
- Weak patterns archive automatically; robust patterns earn higher trust.
- The ecosystem detects missing capabilities (e.g., no Liquidity Organism coverage) and flags them.
- Broker integration, when it arrives, consumes **paper-validated decision packets** — not raw CSVs.

---

## Relationship to Trading AI Today

The existing research chain (momentum continuation, context intelligence, edge discovery, ensemble, evidence engine) is **early TAE 1.0 tissue**. These modules are precursors to formal organisms and the Knowledge Core. Sprint 1 documentation names the architecture they are growing into — without changing live execution paths.

---

## Guiding Question

Before any new module ships, ask:

> *Does this help the ecosystem learn something true, validate it, explain it, and store it — or does it merely generate another number?*

If the answer is only a number, it is not TAE-ready.
